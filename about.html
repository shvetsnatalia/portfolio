A little about my trip to web development.

After working in oil and gas industry, I have decided to change my career path and try something new and my first decision was to learn UX/UI design.

After completing UX/UI bootcamp, I was craving to get better understanding of product development. I wanted to be capable to step out of prototyping box and be able to create the final product writing every letter of the code by myself.

Here in my portfolio you can find selected projects where I have applied my combined experiences of web design and UX/UI.

I keep exploring the artistic power of CSS and continue discovering new beautiful capabilities of JavaScript.
